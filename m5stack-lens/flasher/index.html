🚂 Loco Lens

Flash your M5Stack StopWatch to control a live Lego Loco instance

1 · Flash the firmware

Connect the M5Stack StopWatch over USB-C. Enter download mode first: hold the reset/power button ~2 s until the internal green LED lights, then release. Click below (desktop Chrome/Edge — WebSerial).

Your browser can't flash — use desktop Chrome/Edge. Serial access was blocked — allow the USB port and retry.

Firmware binaries are expected under flasher/firmware/ (produced by pio run + esptool merge-bin). This page ships the manifest + UI; the binaries are added by the release build.

2 · Point it at your cluster

  1. Set Wi-Fi + bridge host in firmware/src/config.h before flashing (or provision over serial after boot).
  2. The watch connects to ws://<bridge>:3001/ws/lens/<instanceId>.
  3. In the dashboard, POST /api/watch/pair to get a pairing code, then redeem it from the watch to bind it to an instance.

3 · Use the Lens

  1. Drag — move the lens over the city.
  2. Tap — inspect the object under the lens centre.
  3. Long-press — close the inspection.
  4. Side buttons — zoom out / in.

The round display shows a live circular crop of the actual game — the crop matches Lego Loco's in-game inspection magnifier.
